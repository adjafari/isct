Research & Development Projects

We partner with industry and academia to develop innovative solutions across a variety of domains. Below are some current and past projects, illustrating the breadth of our activities.

Artificial Intelligence & Software Development

Developing custom AI software for data analysis, optimisation and decision support in health and energy sectors.

Computed Tomography & Imaging

Implementing advanced computed tomography systems and reconstruction algorithms to improve non‑destructive testing and medical diagnostics.

Quantum Algorithms & Machine Learning

Exploring quantum algorithms and quantum machine learning techniques for speed‑ups in optimisation and data processing tasks.

Vibration, Acoustical & Infrared Imaging

Designing and deploying vibration analysis, acoustical imaging and infrared imaging solutions for industrial diagnostics and monitoring.

Epilepsy Prediction System

Developing a friendly wearable system for predicting epileptic seizures using physiological signals and machine learning.

Health Survey System for Large Populations

Creating a scalable health survey platform for monitoring vital signs such as ECG, heart rate, blood pressure, respiration, blood oxygen saturation, blood glucose, perspiration and temperature. The system can be extended to include motion analysis, implantable devices and environmental sensors.
