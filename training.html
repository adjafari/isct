Training Programs

We offer onsite training workshops ranging from introductory to state‑of‑the‑art topics. Each module can be tailored to your audience and delivered at your organisation.

Basics of Bayesian Inference

  • Probability theory fundamentals and Bayes’ rule
  • Bayesian inference for inverse problems
  • Case studies: signal deconvolution and computed tomography

Bayesian Computation Methods

  • Analytic computation for Gaussian models
  • Laplace approximation and variational Bayesian approximation
  • Message passing and approximate message passing algorithms

Data, Signal & Image Processing

  • Data representations: plots, histograms and transforms (Fourier, time–frequency, wavelets)
  • Case studies: interpolation, extrapolation and periodic component estimation
  • General inverse problems and Bayesian inference tools

Inverse Problems in Medical Imaging

  • Overview of imaging systems (CT, MRI, PET, SPECT, etc.)
  • Mathematical foundations: Fourier, Radon, Hilbert and Abel transforms
  • Classical and model‑based iterative methods
  • Bayesian inference and state‑of‑the‑art techniques

Big Data & Deep Learning for Healthcare

  • Introduction to big data and its importance in bioinformatics and health informatics
  • Three‑day workshop covering data pipelines, cloud computing and privacy issues

Bayesian Machine Learning & Mixture Models

  • Parametric modelling and Bayesian parameter estimation
  • Hierarchical models with hidden variables
  • Maximum a posteriori, expected a posteriori and information criteria (AIC, BIC)
  • Variational Bayesian approximation (VBA), expectation–maximisation (EM) and message passing
  • Case studies: clustering and classification using Gaussian and Student‑t mixture models

Deep Learning

  • Introduction to deep learning, transfer learning and reinforcement learning
  • Convolutional and recurrent neural networks for medical imaging
  • Best practices for training and validating deep neural networks
  • Big data management and privacy/security in medical applications

Introduction to Quantum Computing

  • Qubits, quantum gates, and superposition, Entanglement and measurement, Quantum circuits and the Dirac notation
  • Quantum Programming Basics: Simulating quantum circuits, Visualizing quantum states and operations

Quantum Algorithms

  • Deutsch-Jozsa algorithm, Grover’s algorithm, search problems
  • Quantum phase estimation, Quantum Fourier Transform, HHL Algorithm for solving linear systems
  • Applications of Quantum Algorithms: optimization, and cryptography
  • Variational and Hybrid Quantum Algorithms: Variational Quantum Eigensolver (VQE)
  • Quantum Approximate Optimization Algorithm (QAOA)

Quantum Machine Learning

  • Quantum kernels and classification
  • Quantum-enhanced feature spaces
  • Case studies in QML
